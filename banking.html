FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Find quick answers to the most common banking queries.

How soon will my FD be activated after booking?

Fixed Deposits are generally activated within 2 business hours after we receive your confirmation and funds. A digital receipt is sent to your registered email immediately.

Can I modify the amount for my Recurring Deposit later?

Yes. You can enhance or reduce the monthly RD contribution twice during the tenure. The maturity amount will be recalculated instantly based on the revised contribution.

What documents do I need to open a new savings account?

A valid photo ID (Aadhaar, Passport or PAN), proof of address, and a recent passport-sized photograph are required. For salary accounts, an employer introduction letter or salary slip is also requested.

How long does a loan approval take?

For pre-approved customers, approvals are often completed within 24 hours. New-to-bank customers may take 2-4 working days post document submission and verification.
