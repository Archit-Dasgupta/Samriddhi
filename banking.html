FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Tap each question to see the answer. Shareable audio notes are also available inside the app.

How soon will my FD be activated after booking?

Fixed Deposits are usually activated within 2 business hours after we receive funds. A digital receipt arrives on SMS and WhatsApp immediately.

Can I change the amount for my Recurring Deposit later?

Yes. You can increase or reduce the monthly RD contribution two times during the tenure. The maturity amount recalculates instantly in the app.

What documents do I need to open a new savings account?

Aadhaar, PAN (or other photo ID) and one recent photo are enough. If you run a shop, carry any proof like electricity bill or rent receipt.

How long does a loan approval take?

Pre-approved customers get approvals in 24 hours. New applicants usually take 2-4 working days after document pick-up.
