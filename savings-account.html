Common questions

Do I need to visit the branch?

No. Most accounts are opened through video KYC or doorstep visit by a partner bank representative.

How much should I deposit first?

You can start with ₹500. Zero balance is allowed for the first 6 months after activation.

Can I add family members?

Yes. Up to 3 family members can get debit cards at no extra cost. Mention their names in the form.
